Privacy Policy

Effective Date: January 1, 2026

At Famous HD Biryani, we respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you interact with our restaurant, website, or services.

By using our services, you agree to the practices described in this policy.

1. Information We Collect

We may collect the following types of information:

Contact Information: Name, email address, phone number, and billing or delivery address when placing an order or contacting us.

Order Information: Details of orders placed through our website or ordering platforms.

Technical Information: IP address, browser type, device type, and website usage data collected through cookies and analytics tools.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use collected information to:

  • Process and fulfill food orders
  • Provide customer support
  • Send order confirmations and updates
  • Improve our website and services
  • Send promotions or offers if you subscribe to our mailing list

3. Sharing of Information

We only share information with trusted partners that help operate our services, such as:

  • Payment processors
  • Delivery partners
  • Email marketing platforms

We do not sell your personal data. Information may also be disclosed if required by law.

4. Cookies and Tracking

Our website uses cookies and analytics technologies to improve performance and understand visitor behavior. You may disable cookies through your browser settings. However, disabling cookies may affect certain website functions.

5. Marketing Communications

If you subscribe to our newsletter, we may send promotional emails about:

  • Special offers
  • Seasonal menu items
  • Restaurant updates

You may unsubscribe at any time by clicking the unsubscribe link in our emails.

6. Data Security

We implement appropriate technical measures to protect your information from unauthorized access, misuse, or disclosure.

7. Children's Privacy

Our services are not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The latest version will always be posted on this page with the updated effective date.
